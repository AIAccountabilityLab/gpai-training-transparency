## Overview

The AI Act's Article 53(1)(d) requires General-Purpose AI (GPAI) model providers to "make publicly available a sufficiently detailed summary about the content used for training ... according to a template provided by the AI Office". We evaluate the quality for this documentation across two aspects: Transparency and Usefulness and assign a score using our developed methodology. To assist GPAI Providers, the AI Office, and stakeholders, we also work on providing Recommendations.

## FAQ

- *How did you decide what quality means? How did you assign the scores?* Please see our peer-reviewed article and website.
- *We have published/updated our public summary, how will you assess/update the scores?* We have a schedule where we look for new summaries and updates and perform our assessments. Preferably, we'd appreciate if you can [contact](https://aial.ie/research/gpai-training-transparency/about) us directly to let us know about the publisher summary or update. This will also give us the chance to understand your perspectives as the provider and to collate this feedback towards improving future versions of the template.
- *Can I open a github issue for a new summary or update?* Yes! 
- *Can I report a found summary even if I'm not the provider?* Yes!
- *Can I suggest improvements to the framework/methodology/website?* Yes, we welcome all feedback. Please use github issues or [emails](https://aial.ie/research/gpai-training-transparency/about)
- *Do you accept PRs?* No, please check with us before you open any PRs to make sure your contributions and efforts are best placed towards this work.
