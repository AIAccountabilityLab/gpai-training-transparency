Evaluation Notes

The public summary for HuggingFace's SmolLM3-3B model is available on its platform as a <a href="https://huggingface.co/spaces/hfmlsoc/smollm3-eu-data-transparency" target="_blank">webpage</a>. In general, the structure of the template was preserved with clear indication of section numbers, consistent field labels, and explicitly indicating yes/no where applicable. Other than issues with accessibility, we found no major issues with the public summary. It scored <span class="openness open">82.58%</span> and received a grade of <strong>B+</strong> for <strong><i>Transparency</i></strong>, and <span class="openness open">86.01%</span> with grade <strong>B+</strong> for <strong><i>Usefulness</i></strong>, which meant that the summary was of a moderate but good quality. SmolLM scored particularly well on its disclosure of public data sources, a key disclosure demanded by Article 53(1)(d), and a good indicator of transparency in model development process.
