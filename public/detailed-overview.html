Overview of Quality Assessment Findings

Below is an overview of our quality assessment for each public summary. We scored each section of the summary based on our developed methodology, and then calculated the overall score and grade. Each summary is scored for two dimensions: Transparency (T) on how the information is provided, and Usefulness (U) for whether it is sufficient for stakeholders' needs. For convenience, all scores are reflected as a percentage (out of hundred) and the grades are expressed on a scale from A+ (highest) to F (lowest). Public summaries that were missing are marked as "N/A".
