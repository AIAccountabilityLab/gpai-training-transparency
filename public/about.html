About Us & Contact

Contact

We welcome queries, feedback, and collaborations on this work. In particular, please do reach out to us if:

  • You are a GPAI Provider who has published a public summary and wishes to assess its quality, or has revised their public summary and wants us to reassess it, or wants to know how to improve an already published summary.
  • You are a Rights-Exercise Stakeholder who wants to understand how to make use of the quality assessment to understand the public summary.
  • You are a Researcher who has done similar work or wants to do similar work.

To get in touch, use:

  • Github to open issues and discussions
  • E-mail aial@tcd.ie for formal enquiries and administrative matters
  • Contact Dr. Harshvardhan Pandit at harshvardhan.pandit@adaptcentre.ie
  • Contact Dick Blankvoort at dick.blankvoort@ru.nl

About us

Harshvardhan Pandit is a research fellow at the AI Accountability Lab at Trinity College Dublin, specializing in the implications of regulations on technology and how these solve or hinder real-world challenges associated with privacy, legal and regulatory compliance, and consent. His PhD (Computer Science, Trinity College Dublin) explored the application of linked data and semantic web technologies towards GDPR compliance, with a particular focus on consent and provenance.

Dick Blankvoort is a researcher at the European Open Source AI Index and a former visiting researcher at the AI Accountability Lab. His work focuses on the open-source AI and the societal implications of AI systems.

Maximilian Gahntz is the AI Policy lead at Mozilla Foundation.
