The severity represents the extent of the issue, with low indicating aspects that could be fixable, and high representing missing information or requiring major changes.

    • M4 Document does not provide a link to the authoritative source of the document. Date format slightly incorrect. medium

    • M6 Document does not clearly indicate changes from previous version. Document does not indicate where notice of updates or changes will be provided. medium
